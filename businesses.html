Answer the questions investors and partners ask

Not just averages. The analyses owners actually need.

A satisfaction average tells you the score. It does not tell you what predicts retention, which segment is the outlier, or whether the relationship runs through onboarding quality. ReliCheck ships the analyses operators actually need, in plain language, with the reliability checks on the same page.

Readiness

Is this survey ready to send?

The Survey Readiness check scores the design across six weighted domains before a single response arrives. Catches missing constructs, scale-balance issues, and length problems while you can still fix them.

Segments

How does each segment feel?

Per-segment means with 95 percent CI and Cohen's d versus everyone else. K-anonymity hides subgroups below the privacy threshold so a customer cohort of three never appears in a chart.

Drivers

Which factors drive renewal or referral?

The Key Drivers tab ranks every survey factor by its share of explained variance using Johnson's Relative Weights for continuous outcomes (CSAT scores) or standardized log-odds for binary outcomes (renewed yes or no).

Pre/Post

Did the change actually move the needle?

Compare onboarding rebuilds, pricing changes, or product launches across waves. Reliable Change Index reports per-customer movement at 90, 95, and 99 percent confidence so you can defend the claim.

Open-ended

What are customers actually saying?

Theme extraction with sentiment across written responses. Themes are named, counted, and quoted with example responses so the qualitative read sits next to the score.

Strength Index

Can I trust this survey?

The ReliCheck Survey Strength Index™ is a 0-to-100 composite you can paste into a deck. Six weighted domains roll into one verdict band so an investor or partner reads the credibility before reading the result.
