One connected platform

From first question
to defensible conclusion.

Most platforms give you pieces. A form builder here, a stats tool there, a report somewhere else. The work of connecting them — and the risk of something breaking in the gaps — falls on you.

ReliCheck is built as one continuous process. Design your instrument. Check its strength. Analyze the evidence. Produce a report you can defend. Every step earns the next.

01
Quality is checked before you launch The Readiness tab flags weak constructs, missing grouping variables, and item problems before data collection begins.
02
Analysis is guided, not dumped Each studio walks you through the right method for your evidence type — quant, qual, mixed, assessment, or feedback.
03
Reports explain what the evidence can carry Not just what the data says — what it supports, what it doesn't, and what someone can responsibly conclude.
