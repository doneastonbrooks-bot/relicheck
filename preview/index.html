How it works

From question to clear report in four steps

Everything happens in one place. No switching tools, no exporting between systems, no separate stats package to learn.

01

Build your survey

Choose question types, add logic and branding, or start from a validated template. AI question review flags wording problems before respondents see them.

Likert · choice · open-ended · matrix · ranking
02

Collect responses

Share by public link, email list, QR code, or embed. Responses land in your dashboard the moment they're submitted, with completion tracked in real time.

Live counts · partial-save · close on date or sample size
03

Check your data

ReliCheck flags weak scales, low response counts, and missing-data patterns automatically. You see what's solid and what's not before you read the headline.

Reliability check · item flags · sample-size warnings
04

Export your report

Download PDF, Excel, or SPSS. Push results to Google Sheets or Drive in one click. Each report opens with an AI-assisted plain-English summary.

PDF · Excel · SPSS · Google Sheets · Drive

What makes ReliCheck different

Three things you don't get from a generic form tool

QC

Question quality checks AI

Spot double-barreled questions, leading wording, and missing scale anchors before you launch. AI review surfaces the issue and suggests a fix you can accept or edit.

α

Reliability and data-quality flags

Cronbach's alpha, split-half, item-total correlations, and missing-data patterns are computed for every survey. You learn what's solid before you read the headline.

¶

Plain-language reports AI

Each report opens with an AI-assisted summary that names the finding, the sample, and the caveats. Drop it into a deck, share it with a board, defend it in a review.
