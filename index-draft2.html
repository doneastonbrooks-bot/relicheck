What ReliCheck checks

The analyses that decide whether the number holds up.

Every report runs the same battery. Skip the ones that do not apply, defend the ones that do.

Reliability Cronbach's alpha and McDonald's omega per construct, with item-total and alpha-if-deleted flags.
Factor structure EFA and confirmatory CFA on every multi-item construct. Measurement invariance across groups.
Item quality Per-item descriptives, weak-item flags, and AI-suggested rewrites for items that drag the scale down.
Response quality Straight-lining, duplicate vectors, low-effort open-ended answers, and per-channel quality skew.
Open-ended alignment Theme extraction, sentiment, and a check on whether the qualitative answers agree with the numbers.
Equity gaps Per-group means, ANOVA, and pairwise Cohen's d on every detected demographic axis. k-anonymity protects small cells.
Key drivers Standardized regression and Johnson Relative Weights rank what moves your outcome. Action Priority Map names where to fix first.
Pre and post Paired t-test or Wilcoxon, Cohen's d_z, and a Jacobson-Truax Reliable Change Index at 90, 95, and 99 percent confidence.
Readiness check Pre-publish review of the design itself. Items per construct, construct coverage, length sanity, reverse-scoring balance.
