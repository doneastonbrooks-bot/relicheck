What ReliCheck does

Three jobs. One workspace.

Job 01

I have results from another tool and need to check them.

Upload a Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey, Google Forms, or CSV export. ReliCheck scores the data and explains what is strong, what is weak, and what to fix before you share the results.

 Job 02

I need to build a new survey or classroom test.

Start with a validated scale, an AI-suggested item set, or a blank canvas. Every draft gets a pre-publish readiness check so design problems surface before respondents see them.

 Job 03

I need a credibility report a reviewer will accept.

Export a PDF or live dashboard a decision-maker can actually read. Every claim is tied to a number underneath, and every number is explained in plain language.

Four questions ReliCheck answers

The ones reviewers and decision-makers ask first.

Is this survey ready to send?

ReliCheck checks whether your draft has enough items, clear constructs, balanced scales, and obvious design problems before respondents ever see it.

Do the questions work together?

ReliCheck shows whether items in the same scale are moving together, which items weaken the survey, and whether the results are strong enough to interpret.

Are there meaningful group differences?

ReliCheck surfaces important differences across groups while protecting small subgroups from being exposed.

What should we fix first?

ReliCheck identifies the questions and patterns most connected to the outcome you care about, then turns them into clear next steps.
