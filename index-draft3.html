Four questions ReliCheck answers

The ones education and evaluation buyers ask first.

Is this survey ready to send?

A Survey Readiness score grades the design before responses come in. Items per construct, construct coverage, length sanity, and reverse-scoring balance all roll up into one readout.

Do the questions work together?

Cronbach's alpha and McDonald's omega for every construct, with item-total correlations and alpha-if-deleted flags so a weak item never hides inside a strong scale.

Where are the equity gaps?

Per-group means, omnibus ANOVA, and pairwise Cohen's d on every detected demographic axis. Small subgroups stay hidden by k-anonymity; the gaps that matter stay visible.

Which factors matter most?

Key Driver Analysis ranks the items that move the outcome you care about, with an Action Priority Map that names where to fix first and where to protect what is working.
