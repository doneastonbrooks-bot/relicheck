# 360 Subject Brief

**Where it lives:** Top of every 360 subject report; full report also downloadable as a PDF.

**Outcome.** Hand a manager a 360 they can actually use.

A short summary at the top of each subject report names the strongest theme, the biggest development area, and any blind spots, with a one-glance tone pill above the numbers.

**More detail.** The strongest theme gets named by quoting the highest-rated item; the biggest development area by quoting the lowest. Blind spots are flagged when self-ratings exceed others by 0.75 points or more on the five-point scale (or in the reverse direction). The closing line nudges what the manager or HR partner should do next. Tone pills: Strong picture, Solid with notes, Gaps to address, Significant gaps.
