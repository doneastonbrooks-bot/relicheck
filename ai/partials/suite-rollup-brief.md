# Suite Roll-Up Brief

**Where it lives:** Top of the suite detail view. Gated to suites with two or more attached surveys.

**Outcome.** Get the line you would actually read aloud in a leadership meeting.

A quarterly card pins to the top of any suite with two or more attached surveys, with a one-word tone pill and a paragraph that names specific constructs by their label.

**More detail.** The paragraph names specific constructs and surveys ("HR Suite is steady this quarter; engagement is essentially flat, exit volume is up modestly, response volume slipped 12 percent"). Tone pills: Steady or improving, Mixed quarter, Watch this quarter, Slipping. Under the surface: quarter-over-quarter response volume, average Strength Index across the suite, and Likert-mean direction on any construct tagged across two or more surveys. Numbers come from live data, never invented.
