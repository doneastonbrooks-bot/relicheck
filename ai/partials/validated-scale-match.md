# Validated Scale Match

**Where it lives:** Lives in the Questions builder toolbar.

**Outcome.** Use an instrument someone has already validated, whenever one fits.

Describe what you're measuring (resilience in first-year teachers, loneliness in graduate students, perceived stress in oncology nurses). Two to four validated scales come back with the scale name, the construct, the recommended citation, the typical sample size, and a one-sentence rationale. When the recommendation matches ReliCheck's built-in library, one click stands up a starter survey.
