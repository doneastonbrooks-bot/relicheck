# Tab Briefs

**Where it lives:** Top of Description, Validity, Open-Ended, Compare, Pre/Post, Subgroups, and Predictors tabs.

**Outcome.** Open any analytics tab and you'll already know what the page is saying.

A plain-language paragraph sits at the top of the seven everyday tabs. Description covers response patterns and ceiling effects; Validity translates the factor structure; Open-Ended describes engagement without touching respondent text; Compare interprets group differences; Pre/Post explains learning gains; Subgroups names the biggest gaps; Predictors reports what drives the outcome.
