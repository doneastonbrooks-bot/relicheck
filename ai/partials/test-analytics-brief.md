# Test Analytics Brief

**Where it lives:** Top of every test analysis dashboard.

**Outcome.** Read your classroom test the way a department chair would.

The paragraph names the strongest and weakest items by their label, flags possibly miskeyed questions before they cost another semester, and reports whether the test reliably ranked the students. Plain language, no jargon, ready to forward. Under the surface: reliability, pass rate, item difficulty, and item quality.
