# Verdict Cards

**Where it lives:** Top of every analytics tab, above the math card.

**Outcome.** Know whether you can act on what's on the page.

Above the math on every dashboard, a tiered headline ("Strong," "Compare with care," "Not yet ready") and a short action list scoped to that tier.

**More detail.** Same four tiers across every analysis (reliability: Strong / Adequate / Modest / Weak; invariance: Yes / Compare with care / Not on equal footing / Not yet ready). Every body paragraph names the actual outcome and grouping variable, so the read is about your data, not a template. Predictors reads as "A 1-point increase in Manager support is associated with about a 0.4-point increase in Engagement (standardized beta = 0.41)." Mediation, moderation, group comparison, pre/post change, subgroup gaps, key drivers, IRT, and MLM all use the same shape.
