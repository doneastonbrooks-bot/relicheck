# Report Drafter

**Where it lives:** Top of the Strength Index tab.

**Outcome.** Get a starting paragraph in the voice that matches your audience.

One click on the Strength Index turns the live snapshot into a two-to-three paragraph draft you can edit. Researcher voice writes a Methods-and-Results paragraph with alpha, omega, factor count, and cumulative variance cited precisely. HR-lead voice writes a leadership summary with the headline first and the numbers in parentheses. Teacher voice writes a department-friendly paragraph with a specific next step. Numbers come from the snapshot only, never invented.
