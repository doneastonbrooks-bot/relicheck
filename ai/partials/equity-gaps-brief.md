# Equity Gaps Brief

**Where it lives:** Top of the Equity Gaps analytics tab (in More analyses).

**Outcome.** Surface differences across groups in language a leadership team can act on.

The Brief reports outcome differences across protected-class or program groups using the survey's actual labels, framed as patterns to understand, never accusations.

**More detail.** Axes appear by the survey's actual question label; groups appear by their actual option labels; effect sizes get translated into plain English ("engagement is 0.71 standard deviations lower for one group than another on the race or ethnicity axis"). When subgroups were hidden for k-anonymity, the hidden count is noted once so the reader knows a smaller-group story may be missing. Under the surface: Cohen's d, k-anonymity thresholds.
