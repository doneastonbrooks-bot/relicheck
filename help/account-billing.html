Account and billing

Plans, team seats, sharing, invoicing, and discounts.

Team and sharing

How do I add teammates?

Professional and Business plans include team sharing. Invite from Account → Team. Roles include admin, editor, and viewer.

Can I share results with non-account holders?

Yes. Reports can be shared via a read-only link, and PDF / Word / Excel / SPSS exports work for any audience without an account.

Plans and invoicing

How do I change plans?

Go to Account → Billing → Change plan. The change takes effect immediately, with a prorated charge or credit on your next invoice.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Account → Billing → Cancel. Your access continues through the end of the current paid period. Survey content is retained per the retention policy.

Do you offer education or non-profit pricing?

Yes. Educators, students, verified non-profits, and government use cases get up to 30% off all paid tiers. Contact sales for verification and a promo code.

Can I get an invoice instead of paying by card?

Yes, on Business plans. Annual contracts can be invoiced with net-30 or net-45 terms. Email sales@relichecksurvey.com to set up.
