What every ReliCheck report contains

Nine sections, written for the people who have to defend the numbers.

Survey Strength Score

The headline number, status band, and band-specific guidance you can paste into a deck.

Executive summary

AI-assisted plain-language overview of the finding, the sample, and the caveats.

Key findings

Bullets pulled from the strongest signals across reliability, factor structure, response quality, and themes.

Reliability check

Cronbach's alpha, split-half, item-total correlations, and "alpha if deleted" per scale.

Factor analysis summary

Number of factors retained, variance explained, KMO, Bartlett's significance, factor names (AI-suggested, user-editable).

Open-ended themes

Theme names, counts, sentiment, and example quotes pulled from written responses.

Item flags

Items that hurt the scale, items with ceiling or floor effects, items with negative loadings.

Recommended fixes

Per-item revision guidance, scales to split, scales to drop, and decisions you can defend.

Technical appendix

The full math, for the reader who wants it. Reviewers, auditors, and peer readers find what they need.
