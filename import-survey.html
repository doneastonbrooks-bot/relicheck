What happens next

From your survey text to a working ReliCheck survey in three steps

01

Paste or upload

Paste your survey text or drop in a .qsf file. ReliCheck reads it on your behalf without sending any response data to the model.

02

Review the extracted questions

Confirm question types, anchors, options, and reverse-coded items before anything gets created. Edit anything that looks off.

03

Open in the builder

The survey opens in ReliCheck's builder ready for AI question review, distribution, and the Survey Strength Score once responses start landing.
