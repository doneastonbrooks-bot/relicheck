FAQ

Common questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and you're prorated for the rest of the billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current period.

Is there an academic or nonprofit discount?

Researcher pricing already reflects an academic-friendly rate. For department-wide or nonprofit licensing, contact us and we'll work out a fit.

What happens to my surveys if I downgrade?

Your data stays. Surveys above your new plan's limits move to read-only until you upgrade or remove them. Reports remain accessible at all times.

Do you offer a free trial of paid features?

Sign up free and try the Researcher tier with a promo code. We give every new account 30 days of Researcher-tier access on request, no credit card required.

Can I pay annually?

Yes. Annual billing saves about 17% across all paid plans. Toggle the switch above the price grid to see annual rates.
